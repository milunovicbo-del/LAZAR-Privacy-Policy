Email address
Password
Name and surname
Age

We do not collect any health, biometric, or financial data through the App.
We do not integrate any third‑party analytics, crash‑reporting, payment processors, or AI APIs at this time.
3. How we use your data
Your personal data is used for the following purposes:
To create and manage your user account.
To enable you to synchronise your habit‑tracking data across devices.
To send essential service‑related messages (e.g. account verification, security alerts, or important updates).
To improve the App and understand how it is used (only in an aggregated, non‑identifiable way where possible).
To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights or the rights of others.
We do not use your data for targeted advertising or profiling.
4. Legal basis for processing (where applicable)
Because LAZAR is a global app, and you may be in different jurisdictions, we generally rely on the following bases where relevant:
Contract performance: To provide the App and your account.
Legitimate interest: To improve the App and ensure security and proper functioning.
Consent (where required): We will only process sensitive data if you explicitly consent and can withdraw consent at any time.
We will always comply with applicable data‑protection laws in any country you are accessing LAZAR from.
5. Data sharing and disclosure
We do not share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or profiling purposes.
We may disclose your data only in the following limited circumstances:
To comply with a legal obligation (e.g. responding to a court order, law‑enforcement request, or regulatory authority).
To protect our rights, safety, or the rights of others.
If our business is sold or reorganised, your data may be transferred to the new owner, but only for the same purposes described in this Privacy Policy.
We do not use any third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs, so your data is not shared with advertisers or data‑brokers.
6. Data security
We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, or alteration.
However, no system is 100% secure, and you should keep your account details confidential and use strong passwords.
7. Data retention
We will only keep your personal data for as long as necessary to:
Deliver the services you use in LAZAR.
Meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.
If you delete your account or request deletion of your data, we will erase your information unless we are required by law to keep it.
8. Your rights (for users in regulated regions)
If you are in a country with data‑protection laws (e.g. those based on GDPR or similar frameworks), you may have the right to:
Request access to your personal data.
Correct inaccurate information.
Request erasure or restriction of processing.
Object to or withdraw consent for certain types of processing.
You can exercise these rights by contacting us at support@growthlabapps.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe in line with applicable law.
If you are in Tanzania, we will still aim to respond promptly and handle your requests fairly.
9. Children’s privacy
LAZAR is not intended for use by minors. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it promptly.
10. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or an in‑app notice, and the updated policy will apply once you continue using the App after the notice period.
Your continued use of LAZAR after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
